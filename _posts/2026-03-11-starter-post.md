Welcome to my blog(?), which will also be used for the purpose of posting and documenting some random things that I (will, hopefully) build! This is a starter post to get things going.

I don't expect any of these posts to be read (or tools, used), but I suppose there is an implied sense of accountability in posting something to the public, as compared to, say, a personal journal entry, or a hastily written markdown note. While the latter forms of "sloppiness" are certainly fine, it has occured to me that I now seldom have the opportunity to write "seriously" (as compared to when I was an undergrad), and it might be admirable to try and retain whatever written voice and rhetorical capability I have presently, as hopeless as that may be, given my chosen profession.
